Speechify WASM Demo

In-house pure-C front-end + unit-selection synth, compiled to WebAssembly. The Tom voice corpus (~92 MB) is loaded once from a preloaded virtual filesystem.

Building blocks: Tom 3.0.5 voice corpus, in-house pure-C FE (POS tagging, syllabification, prosody), USel viterbi DP, WSOLA concatenative synth. No network calls after the initial asset load; all synthesis is local in the browser.

Speechify WASM Demo

Emulator-backed SWIttsFe front-end + unit-selection synth, compiled to WebAssembly. Voices are downloaded on demand — pick one and its corpus streams into the browser; nothing is bundled up front.

Loading WebAssembly module...

Building blocks: Speechify 3.0.5 voice corpora, emulator-backed SWIttsFe front-end (POS tagging, syllabification, prosody), USel viterbi DP, WSOLA concatenative synth. A voice is fetched once, then cached by the browser; all synthesis runs locally — no network calls after a voice is loaded.
